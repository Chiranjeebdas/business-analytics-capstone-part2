# Recommendation Memo

## Subject

Recommendation Based on A/B Experiment Results

---

## Executive Summary

An A/B experiment was conducted to evaluate whether the new product experience (Treatment) performs better than the existing experience (Control).

The analysis compared key business metrics including:

- Paid Conversion Rate
- Revenue
- Engagement Score
- Refund Rate
- Support Ticket Rate
- Days to Convert

---

## Key Findings

- Conversion performance was compared between Control and Treatment groups.
- User engagement was evaluated using Engagement Score.
- Customer satisfaction was monitored using Refund Rate and Support Ticket Rate.
- Revenue performance was measured using 30-day Revenue.

---

## Recommendation

If the Treatment group demonstrates:

- Higher Paid Conversion Rate
- Equal or Higher Revenue
- No increase in Refund Rate
- No increase in Support Tickets
- Equal or Better Engagement

then the Treatment experience should be rolled out to all users.

Otherwise, additional experimentation is recommended before a full rollout.

---

## Business Impact

Expected benefits include:

- Higher customer conversion
- Increased revenue
- Better user engagement
- Improved customer experience
- Data-driven decision making

---

## Final Recommendation

Proceed with rollout only if statistical significance and business guardrails are satisfied.
